# Roadmap - Engineering Computation Notebooks

## V2.0: Deployable (Make it Work)
- **Objective**: Full FE exam topic coverage with computational notebooks.
- **Key Features**:
  - Comprehensive notebooks for Circuits, Dynamics, Fluids, Statics, and Thermo.
  - Use of `numpy` and `sympy` for symbolic and numerical solutions.
  - Detailed explanations and walkthroughs for each topic.
  - Open-source repository as a portfolio of foundational engineering mastery.

## V3.0: Monetizable (Make it Earn)
- **Objective**: "Pass the FE Exam with Python" online course.
- **Key Features**:
  - Launch on platforms like Teachable or Udemy.
  - Interactive notebooks paired with high-quality video walkthroughs.
  - Tiered pricing ($49-99) for different levels of access.
  - Exclusive community for exam prep support.

## Version N: Scaled Monopoly (Make it Scale)
- **Objective**: Autonomous AI engineering tutor.
- **Key Features**:
  - AI agents for personalized learning paths and problem generation.
  - Real-time grading and feedback on student solutions.
  - Adaptive difficulty levels based on performance metrics.
  - Integration with professional licensing boards and exam bodies.
